Our Mission

At the Computer Science Club, our mission is to inspire interest in programming throughout the community by providing real-world coding experience to students. We strive to foster a collaborative learning environment to help students grow no matter their technical background.

Meeting Information

Meetings are held every Tuesday after school from 3:15-4:15 in Mr. Ware's room 1047 (engineering pod). We do have access to the school computers, but it is recommended to bring your own personal laptop if you have one. Follow our Remind to stay updated on meeting dates.

Innovate, Collaborate, Create

Founded in 2023, the ERHS Computer Science Club is a community dedicated to inspiring a passion for technology and fostering an inclusive environment.

What We Do

  • Engage members in creative and innovative projects to develop games, apps, and websites
  • Provide lessons to help students develop technical skills
  • Offer a supportive community where members can connect, share ideas, and collaborate with others
